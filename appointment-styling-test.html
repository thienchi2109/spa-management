Design Principles Applied

✅ Harmonious and Professional

Unified light green background (#E8F5E9) for all appointment blocks creates visual consistency.

✅ Clear and Easily Recognizable

Left border colors (Green: Scheduled, Blue: Completed, Red: Cancelled) provide instant status recognition.

✅ Information-Focused

Patient names and times are prominent on subtle backgrounds, not overshadowed by bold colors.

✅ Modern and Flexible

Subtle accents with opacity effects and icons create a modern, scalable design system.
